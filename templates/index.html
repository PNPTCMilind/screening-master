About the Score

The StartupHealthScore is an assessment of a startup's potential success based on various metrics and classifications.

Metrics Explained:

  • Prediction: Indicates whether the startup is more likely to succeed (1) or not (0) based on the model.
  • Confidence: Shows the confidence level of the prediction as a percentage, ranging from red (low confidence) to green (high confidence).

Classifications:

  • FR/AC/IP: Indicates if the startup falls into categories of 'FR', 'AC', or 'IP'.
  • CL/NE: Indicates if the startup falls into categories of 'CL' or 'NE'.
